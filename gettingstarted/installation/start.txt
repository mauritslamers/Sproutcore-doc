======= Installing SproutCore ======

There are a couple of ways of installing SproutCore. The way depends of course on what platform you want to install SproutCore, but also what kind of build tools you want to install.
Currently there are two sets of build tools: 
  * abbot: runs in Ruby
  * garçon: runs in Node-JS
